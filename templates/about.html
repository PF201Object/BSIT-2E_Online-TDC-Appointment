Frequently Asked Questions

📋 What are the requirements for TDC?
Student Permit Requirements:
• At least 16 years old (with parent's consent if below 18)
• Valid government-issued ID (Passport, Postal ID, Voter's ID, etc.)
• PSA Birth Certificate (original and photocopy)
• Medical Certificate from an LTO-accredited clinic
• Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN) - optional but recommended
For TDC Course:
• Basic reading and writing skills
• Access to device with internet connection
• Valid email address and mobile number

TDC Course Prices:

⏰ How long is TDC?
The Theoretical Driving Course (TDC) consists of 15 hours of comprehensive training:

• 5 hours - Self-paced online modules (can be completed anytime)
• 10 hours - Live virtual classroom sessions with instructors

Students can complete the course within 1-2 weeks depending on their availability. The certificate is valid for 1 year from the date of completion.
🚗 What are the requirements for PDC?
PDC Requirements:
• Valid Student Permit (from LTO)
• Completed TDC Certificate (must be presented)
• At least 16 years old
• Basic knowledge of traffic signs and rules

Note: You must have passed TDC for the same vehicle type you want to take PDC in.
💰 How much does PDC cost?
PDC Course Prices:
• Motorcycle (2 wheels): ₱1,200.00
• Tricycle (3 wheels): ₱1,350.00
• Car/SUV (4 wheels): ₱1,500.00
• Truck/Bus (6+ wheels): ₱2,000.00

What's included: Vehicle rental, fuel, instructor fee, and PDC certificate.
Note: PDC must be taken for the SAME vehicle type as your TDC.
⏰ How long is PDC?
The Practical Driving Course (PDC) consists of 8 hours of actual behind-the-wheel training:

• Can be taken in multiple sessions (e.g., 2 hours/day over 4 days)
• Flexible scheduling available (Monday-Saturday, 8 AM - 5 PM)
• Includes practical driving on public roads
• Certificate issued upon completion
🔄 Can I take PDC right after TDC?
Yes, but first you need to get your Student Permit from LTO!

Process:
1. Complete TDC course ✅
2. Apply for Student Permit at any LTO office (present your TDC certificate)
3. Get your Student Permit (valid for 1 year)
4. Now you can enroll in PDC!

We recommend booking your Student Permit appointment immediately after completing TDC.
✅ Is the certificate LTO-accredited?
YES! Both TDC and PDC certificates from EcoDrive are fully accredited by the Land Transportation Office (LTO).

TDC Certificate: Required for Student Permit application
PDC Certificate: Required for Non-Professional Driver's License application

📅 How do I schedule my TDC exam?
Scheduling is easy! Follow these steps:

1. Complete all 15 hours of TDC material
2. Pass the module quizzes (required score: 80%)
3. Choose your preferred exam date from available slots
4. Take the final online exam (proctored)
5. Receive your certificate immediately upon passing

Exams are available Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM. Results are instant!

🎓 What's next after TDC?
After completing TDC, follow this path:

Step 1: Apply for Student Permit at any LTO office (bring your TDC certificate)
Step 2: Enroll in Practical Driving Course (PDC) with us
Step 3: Complete 8 hours of PDC training
Step 4: Take the LTO Practical Driving Exam
Step 5: Get your Non-Professional Driver's License! 🎉

We offer special discounts for TDC graduates enrolling in PDC!
🚦 What's the LTO exam like?
LTO Written Exam (for Student Permit):
• 40 questions on traffic rules and road signs
• Need 80% to pass (32 correct answers)
• Our TDC course covers everything you need!

LTO Practical Exam (for Driver's License):
• Actual driving test with LTO officer
• Parallel parking, backing up, turning, road driving
• Our PDC course prepares you specifically for this!
